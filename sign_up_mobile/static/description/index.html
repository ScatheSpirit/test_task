Mobile field for signup

Authorization form with an additional field 'Mobile'

Setting

  • Go to 'Settings' > 'General Settings'

  • In the "Permissions" section, for the 'Customer Account' item, select "Free sign up".

  • Let your customers log in to see their documents

Fields

Add field 'Mobile' to Authorization form

It's also added to the 'Account detail' form.

And accordingly gets into the contact related with the user.
